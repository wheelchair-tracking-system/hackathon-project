JMHC Management Portal is an advanced system designed to streamline hospital wheelchair management. It allows staff to dispense, track, and manage wheelchairs efficiently, ensuring optimal utilization and availability for patients when needed.

To use the Request Wheelchair option, simply enter the patient's ID into the provided input field and click the "Request Wheelchair" button. The system will automatically assign an available wheelchair to the patient and update its status in the portal.

Wheelchairs can be tracked in real-time using the GPS tracking feature integrated into the system. Their current location is displayed on the portal, ensuring quick accessibility and efficient management.

If no wheelchairs are currently available, you can monitor the portal for real-time updates as wheelchairs are returned. Additionally, notify the hospital staff to prioritize the return of in-use wheelchairs for urgent cases.
